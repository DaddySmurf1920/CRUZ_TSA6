Batman

Bruce Wayne

Batman, a.k.a. the Dark Knight, is a vigilante superhero who protects Gotham City from criminals. Born into the wealthy Wayne family, Bruce Wayne witnessed the murder of his parents as a child, which motivated him to dedicate his life to fighting crime. He trained himself to peak physical and mental condition and adopted the persona of Batman, using his intellect, detective skills, martial arts prowess, and high-tech gadgets to combat injustice and strike fear into the hearts of evildoers.

Nightwing

Dick Grayson

Nightwing is the superhero identity adopted by Dick Grayson, formerly known as Robin, after he outgrows the role. As the first Robin, Dick Grayson fought alongside Batman as his trusted sidekick, learning valuable skills and lessons in crimefighting. Eventually, he became independent and assumed the mantle of Nightwing, establishing himself as a formidable hero in his own right. Nightwing is known for his acrobatic prowess, strategic thinking, and unwavering dedication to justice.

Red Hood

Jason Todd

Red Hood is the alias of Jason Todd, the second Robin, after he is resurrected and becomes an antihero. Initially, Jason Todd served as Batman's second sidekick, but he met a tragic fate at the hands of the Joker. However, he was later revived and took on the persona of Red Hood, adopting a more ruthless and lethal approach to crimefighting. Red Hood operates outside the traditional boundaries of heroism, willing to use extreme methods to achieve his goals.

Robin

Tim Drake

Robin is the superhero identity adopted by Tim Drake, a skilled detective and martial artist who partners with Batman. After deducing the identities of Batman and Robin, Tim Drake earns the Dark Knight's trust and becomes the third Robin. As Robin, Tim balances his crimefighting duties with his personal life, displaying intelligence, agility, and courage in battling Gotham's villains. He brings his own unique style to the role of Robin, contributing to the dynamic duo's success in protecting the city.

Scarecrow

Jonathan Crane

Scarecrow is the alias of Jonathan Crane, a deranged psychologist who uses fear toxins to induce terror in his victims. Obsessed with the concept of fear, Jonathan Crane develops a fear-inducing gas and adopts the persona of Scarecrow to study its effects on the human mind. As Scarecrow, he becomes a recurring adversary of Batman, using his knowledge of psychology and chemistry to instill fear in Gotham's citizens. Despite his lack of superhuman abilities, Scarecrow's mastery of fear makes him a formidable foe for the Dark Knight.
