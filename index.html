Batman

Bruce Wayne

Batman, a.k.a. the Dark Knight, is a vigilante superhero who protects Gotham City from criminals.

Nightwing

Dick Grayson

Nightwing is the superhero identity adopted by Dick Grayson, formerly known as Robin, after he outgrows the role.

Red Hood

Jason Todd

Red Hood is the alias of Jason Todd, the second Robin, after he is resurrected and becomes an antihero.

Robin

Tim Drake

Robin is the superhero identity adopted by Tim Drake, a skilled detective and martial artist who partners with Batman.

Scarecrow

Jonathan Crane

Scarecrow is the alias of Jonathan Crane, a deranged psychologist who uses fear toxins to induce terror in his victims.
